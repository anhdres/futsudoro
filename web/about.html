Futsudoro

Futsudoro is a train-inspired pomodoro timer designed for deep work with station-by-station progress.

train pomodoro timer flow-friendly pomodoro chronos kairos timer

Two time modes

  • Chronos (Χρόνος): strict timing. Work and break phases advance automatically.
  • Kairos (Καιρός): flow-friendly timing. Alarms notify phase end, but you decide when to move forward.

Why it exists

Classic pomodoro is great for starting focus, but can interrupt flow. Futsudoro keeps the ritual while respecting momentum.

How sessions work

  • Pick a line and stations.
  • Travel through work and break cycles.
  • Track today and all-time minutes in your travel diary.
